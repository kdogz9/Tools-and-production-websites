Figma

Figma acts as an online whiteboard where you can put all of your research into.

  • It is a collaborative software which allows everyone invited to work on the whiteboard and also see all of the added content by other team memebers.
  • This documentation is very important to keep up to date with as everyone can be kept in the loop in terms of artists research, design concepts and mechanics.
    • Link to Figma board !

ClickUp

ClickUp is a project management platform which allows teams to manage tasks, collaborate and streamline workflows in one place.

  • It includes handy tools such as scheduling for when a task is due, assigning tasks, setting status of tasks, whiteboard feature and a google docs feature.
  • It is really important to keep ClickUp up to date to increase communication and reduce confusion and any drawbacks which could impact the project.
  • Any emails added to the ClickUp need to follow this format: StudentNumber@students.ucreative.ac.uk , in order to work properly.
    • Link to a ClickUp breakdown video !
